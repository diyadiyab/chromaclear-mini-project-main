  • FEATURES
    1. ENHANCE IMAGE: Input image from gallery and enhance it so that colorblind person can distinguish colors.
    2. COLOR IDENTIFICATION USING CURSOR: When cursor is moved over the image, color at that particular point is identified and displayed.
    3. REAL TIME IMAGE AND VIDEO CAPTURE: Allows user to capture images and videos through device camera and image enhancement is done so that people with color blindness can distinguish colors.
    4. EXTENSION FOR COLORBLIND USERS: Converts webpages so that color blind users can distinguish the colors they view on a webpage depending upon their type of color blindness.
    5. EXTENSION FOR DEVELOPERS: Converts webpages so that developers can see how a color-blind person views webpages. This is for knowledge and education of the developers.
  • HELP
    1. ENHANCE IMAGE:
      1. Click on enhance image button at the top.
      2. You will be directed to a new page.
      3. Click the choose file button.
      4. Choose input image from gallery. It will be shown on the first box.
      5. Click convert button.
      6. The converted image is displayed on the second box.
      7. Place the cursor anywhere on the image. Color at that particular point will be displayed.
    2. REAL TIME CAPTURE:
      1. Click on real time capture button at the top.
      2. You will be directed to a new page.
      3. Capture image from camera or take a video using camera on the device.
      4. The converted image or video will be displayed.
    3. EXTENSION:
      1. Click on extension button.
      2. Choose whether you are a user or developer.
      3. Depending on your choice, the 2 extensions will be displayed.
